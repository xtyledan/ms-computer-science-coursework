# CS-530 Artificial Intelligence Principles and Applications

## Course Overview

CS-530 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Principles and Applications explores how artificial intelligence systems are applied across disciplines and the underlying approaches used to build, evaluate, and deploy modern AI-driven solutions. The course focuses on understanding how AI models process data, support decision-making, and enable applications across a wide range of domains.

Modern AI systems operate in complex environments. Applications may integrate machine learning models, interact with large datasets, and deliver functionality across platforms and services. Understanding how AI systems are designed and applied is critical for developing effective, scalable solutions.

## Real-World Context

Artificial intelligence knowledge becomes especially important when systems must operate across diverse use cases and industries. For example, AI models may be used in applications such as natural language processing and computer vision, requiring developers to evaluate performance, accuracy, and applicability in different contexts.

In modern architectures, this complexity increases significantly. AI systems are often deployed within cloud-based environments and integrated into applications that serve users across multiple platforms. These environments combine data processing, model training, and system integration to deliver intelligent functionality at scale.

Understanding how AI systems are developed, evaluated, and applied is essential to building solutions that function effectively in these environments.

## Course Approach

This course approaches artificial intelligence concepts in incremental steps. Complex ideas are broken down into smaller components to build understanding gradually, then combined to form a complete view of how AI systems operate and are applied.

Key topics include:

- Applications of artificial intelligence across disciplines  
- Prompt engineering techniques  
- Ethical considerations in AI systems  
- Machine learning and deep learning fundamentals  
- Evaluation of AI tools and outputs  

## Tools and Technologies

Python-based environments and AI tools are used throughout the course to explore artificial intelligence concepts. Students interact with platforms and resources that support experimentation with AI models and workflows.

The course also incorporates tools such as zyBooks for guided learning, Codio for development environments, and Git and GitHub for version control and project work.
